4) It is perfect trek for beginners, can be done in 2-3 days only. It is easy to moderate trek. It starts from Sankri Village in uttarkashi district. It takes aroud 3 hours to reach at 1st camp site which can be Juda-Ka-Taal or Hargaon, both are at almost same altitude. It take next around 1.5 hours to reach Kedarkantha Base Camp, which can be done in same day easily. But it is advisable to stay at 1st camp site for beginners to get acclimatize properly and to avoid AMS. It takes 3-4 hours to reach summit point from base camp. It is high advisable to leave base camp by 3am in the morning to enjoy beautiful sunrise at top point. A mesmerizing sunrise is the key factor for this trek. It takes aroud 2 hours to reach base camp and it takes further 45min to reach hargaon and further 2 hours to reach sankri village. It is recommended not to stay at any camp while returning back, if you have reached at summit point by sunrise. It is charming only in snow, so best time is December to March. Carry heavy woolen for summit day trek, there should be high quality wind proof jacket.
